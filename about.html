CK & IJ Trading & Projects is a 100% Black-owned enterprise with 51% black female ownership. Established in 2014 as a family-owned business, our organization is fully compliant with South African business regulations and committed to delivering exceptional service.

We aim to empower communities while offering reliable and efficient solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients across diverse industries — from deep-pit mining operations to precision tyre maintenance.
