Our Story

A Family-Built
Legacy of Power
and Precision

CK & IJ Trading & Projects is a 100% Black-owned enterprise with 51% black female ownership. Established in 2014 as a family-owned business, we are fully compliant with South African business regulations and committed to delivering exceptional service across mining and tyre specialist sectors. We aim to empower communities while offering reliable, efficient solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients.
